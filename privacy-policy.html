Privacy Policy for ReelsSaver

At Reelssaver, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Reelssaver and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimizing data collection. The core functionality of Reelssaver—downloading public content from Instagram—does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

Personal Information: We only collect personal information (such as your name or email address) if you voluntarily provide it to us, for example, by contacting us directly at reelssaver12@gmail.com for support or inquiries.

Downloaded Content: We do not host, store, or save any of the videos, photos, or audio files you download using our tool. All media is fetched directly from the original source servers and delivered to your device.

2. Log Files

Reelssaver follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit the website. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Reelssaver uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

4. Advertising Partners

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (known as DART cookies) to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

Our advertising partners may also use cookies and web beacons to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

5. GDPR and CCPA Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email provided below.

6. Children's Information

Reelssaver does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
