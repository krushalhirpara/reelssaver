Frequently Asked Questions
Have a question? Find answers below.
Can I download posts from private accounts? ▾
No, ReelsSaver only works for public Instagram posts. Private account posts are not publicly accessible, so they cannot be downloaded.
Why do I get an "Invalid URL" error? ▾
It is important to select the correct tab. Use the 🎬 Reel tab for Reel URLs (/reel/) and the 📁 Post tab for Post URLs (/p/). Pasting a URL into the wrong tab will cause an error.
Can I download carousels (multiple photos)? ▾
Yes! Paste the /p/ link in the 📁 Post tab and click "Download Photo(s)". When there are multiple photos, a grid will appear — tap on any photo to download it individually.
How do I download a video post in the Post tab? ▾
Paste the URL in the 📁 Post tab. Both "Download Video" and "Download Photo(s)" buttons will appear. Click "Download Video" for videos and "Download Photo(s)" for photos.
What formats are the downloads in? ▾
Videos are downloaded in .mp4 format, photos in .jpg, and thumbnails in .jpg or .webp — all in the best available quality.
